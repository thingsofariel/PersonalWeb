Stepping into the Tech World: My Journey as an Undana Computer Science Graduate

Completing my Computer Science degree at Universitas Nusa Cendana (Undana) has been an incredible milestone in my life. Over the past few years, I have immersed myself in coding languages, software architecture, and problem-solving methodologies that have thoroughly shaped my analytical mindset. The rigorous academic environment and collaborative tech projects at Undana not only provided me with a solid theoretical foundation but also fueled my passion for building impactful digital solutions.

As a fresh graduate, transitioning from academic theories to real-world applications is both thrilling and challenging. During my time at university, I spent countless hours debugging code, collaborating on group development projects, and exploring modern web technologies. These experiences taught me that software engineering is not just about writing clean lines of code, but also about continuous learning, adaptability, and effectively communicating with a team to solve complex problems.

Looking ahead, I am eager to bring my fresh perspective, technical skills, and enthusiastic work ethic into the professional tech industry. I am highly motivated to contribute to innovative projects, continue learning from experienced mentors, and further refine my development capabilities. This blog page marks the beginning of my professional portfolio, where I look forward to sharing my growth, projects, and insights as I embark on this exciting career path.

A Day in the Life: My Professional Journey at Fortuna

Working at Fortuna has been an enriching experience that continuously challenges me to grow as a tech professional. Every day brings a dynamic mix of collaborative brainstorming sessions, hands-on development, and strategic problem-solving. Being part of a fast-paced environment allows me to apply my technical knowledge directly to live projects, ensuring that our digital platforms remain robust, efficient, and user-friendly.

A typical day starts with a team sync-up where we align on our goals, discuss ongoing tasks, and address any potential roadblocks. I spend a significant portion of my time writing clean code, reviewing pull requests, and optimizing our web applications to deliver the best possible user experience. What I enjoy most about Fortuna is the emphasis on teamwork and innovation; everyone is encouraged to propose new ideas and experiment with modern tools to enhance our workflow.

Reflecting on my time here, the learning curve has been rewarding, and the supportive culture at Fortuna has made a significant impact on my career development. Balancing daily deliverables with long-term project goals has taught me invaluable lessons in time management and agile methodologies. I am incredibly proud of what we are building here, and I look forward to tackling more exciting tech challenges with this amazing team.
